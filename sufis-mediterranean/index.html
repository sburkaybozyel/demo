Our Story

Mediterranean Soul in the Heart of Memphis

Sufi's Mediterranean Grill & Bar brings the bold, aromatic flavors of the Mediterranean and Persia to Germantown. From slow-roasted shawarma to hand-rolled grape leaves, every dish is a celebration of authentic tradition.

Come for the food, stay for the atmosphere. Our spacious porch, vibrant decor, and live belly dance shows on Friday and Saturday evenings make every visit a true cultural experience.
