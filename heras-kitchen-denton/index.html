About Us

Three Continents.
One Kitchen.

Hera's Kitchen is Denton's most distinctive dining experience. Drawing on the bold, layered traditions of African and Caribbean cooking and grounding them in American comfort food - the result is a menu that surprises people every single visit.

From the jollof rice that keeps regulars coming back to the garlic fries seasoned with a Kenyan spice blend that customers cannot stop talking about - every plate at Hera's carries a story. Fresh ingredients, daily specials, and the kind of cooking that makes Denton a more interesting city.
