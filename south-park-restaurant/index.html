Our Story

A Clarksville Institution Since 1964

South Park Restaurant has been feeding families, truckers, and travelers for over 60 years. Step inside and you are greeted by a magnificent display cabinet of freshly baked pies and cakes, a sure sign that this is the real deal.

From hearty breakfasts starting at 6 AM to full dinner plates with your choice of sides, we serve the kind of food that brings people back again and again. Ranked #1 in Clarksville by TripAdvisor and Restaurant Guru.
