About Us

Family-Owned.
Community-Loved.

Lynx Restobar is a Black, family-owned and operated restaurant and bar in Shreveport, Louisiana. Built on the foundation of great food, genuine hospitality, and a cozy atmosphere that feels nothing like a chain.

Regulars come for the catfish — described as the best they have ever had. They stay for the wings, the po'boys, the friendly staff, and an atmosphere that makes every visit feel like a neighborhood gathering. A proud participant in Shreveport-Bossier Black Restaurant Week.
