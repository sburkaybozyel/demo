About Us

The Best Mexican
in Clarksville

El Chubasco is not just a Mexican restaurant — it is the Mexican restaurant in Clarksville. With a 4.9 Google rating built from hundreds of loyal customers, this place has earned its reputation the old-fashioned way: by being consistently, undeniably delicious.

Authentic carne asada tacos, quesadillas smothered in green sauce, spicy camarones a la diabla, and eight varieties of fresh salsa made daily. Generous portions, quality ingredients, and prices that make sense. This is the real thing.
