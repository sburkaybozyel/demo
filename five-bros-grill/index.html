About Us

A Family Operation.
Serious About Food.

Five Bros Grill started as one of Northwest Louisiana's best-kept secrets — a family-run grill tucked away in Minden that quickly earned a reputation for some of the boldest, most satisfying food in the region.

Two fresh beef patties smashed on the griddle. Nashville-style hot chicken loaded with pickles and the house Eddie sauce. Loaded fries that customers call "the perfect combination of textures and tastes." The secret is out — and it is spreading. Five Bros is now expanding to Bossier City.
