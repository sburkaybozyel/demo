Our Story

Nearly 30 Years of
Chinese Home Cooking

Since 1996, The Wok has been serving Vicksburg the kind of Chinese food that feels like home. No gimmicks, no frills — just fresh, generous portions at prices that make sense.

Tucked inside Vicksburg Mall, it has become a local institution. Regulars say no trip to Vicksburg would be complete without stopping in for the fried rice. Once you try it, you will understand why.
