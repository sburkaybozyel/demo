About Us

The Rice Family
Feeds Pine Bluff

Rice First Class Catering was founded by the Rice family to bring honest, generous, home-style Southern cooking to Pine Bluff and beyond. From brisket slow-cooked to perfection to soul food plates that feel like Sunday dinner, everything is made with love and intention.

Whether you are stopping in for a plate or booking catering for your next event, the Rice family treats every meal like it matters. Because it does.
