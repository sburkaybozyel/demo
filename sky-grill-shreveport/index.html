About Us

A Mother & Son
Running Something Special

Sky Grill is owned and operated by Brenda Pettigrew and her son, Chef Tim Chester — a family with a passion for quality food and genuine hospitality. Tucked inside Shreveport's historic Downtown Airport terminal, it is unlike any restaurant in the region.

Everything is made from scratch. Bread baked fresh. Homemade ice cream. Caramel banana bread pudding that reviewers cannot stop talking about. Rated 3 Forks by the Shreveport-Bossier Journal and 4.7 stars on Google — this is a place that earns its reputation every single day.

One of a Kind

There Is No Other
Place Like This

Shreveport Downtown Airport has been open since 1931. The terminal was rebuilt in 1971. Sky Grill is the only restaurant where you can eat a handmade burger and watch a plane taxi past your window — no ticket, no credentials, just walk in.
