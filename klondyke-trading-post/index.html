Our Story

Where Vicksburg
Comes to Eat

Housed in a converted two-bay gas station dating back to the 1930s, the Klondyke Trading Post is not just a restaurant — it is a Vicksburg institution. The building itself carries stories older than most of us, and the food has been feeding families, locals, and travelers for generations.

Featured on Andrew Zimmern's "Appetite for Life," the Klondyke has earned its reputation the old-fashioned way: one incredible plate at a time.
