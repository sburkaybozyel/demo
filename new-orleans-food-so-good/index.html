About Us

Real Louisiana
Cooking, Right Here

New Orleans Food So Good brings the bold, soulful flavors of Louisiana straight to Pine Bluff, Arkansas. Every dish is made with care and tradition, from a gumbo that has become a local legend to jambalaya that fills the room with the unmistakable smell of the bayou.

This is a place where the portions are generous, the staff knows your name, and every plate tells you exactly why the name says it all. Come hungry. Leave happy.
