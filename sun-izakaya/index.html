Our Story

A Family's Journey
From East to West

Sun Izakaya was born from a dream. Sam Sun and Yuki Lin — immigrants who arrived in the United States in 2008 and made Vicksburg their home in 2017 — opened this restaurant as a celebration of the flavors and traditions they carried with them.

Drawing on the izakaya spirit — a place to relax, share small plates, and linger over good food — they've crafted a menu that bridges Japanese precision, Korean warmth, and bold fusion creativity. Every dish is made with intention, every meal a story.
