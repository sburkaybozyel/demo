About Us

North Platte's Favorite Bar & Grill

Locora Grill is where North Platte comes to eat, drink, and have a great time. With 17 big screens covering every game, a full bar stocked with draft beers and cocktails, and a menu built around crowd favorites, there is always something going on here.

From hand-tossed pizzas and loaded wings to Philly steak sandwiches and fresh burgers, we make food worth coming back for. Pool tables, gaming machines, karaoke nights, and live music round out the experience.
