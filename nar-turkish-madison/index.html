Our Story

A Dream Named
After Love

Chef Cagri Topcu has been cooking in restaurant kitchens since the 1990s — from his father's table in Turkey to Anatolia Restaurant in New York to a food truck in Los Angeles. When his wife Nar came to Madison for her master's degree at UW, Cagri finally made his lifelong dream real.

He named the restaurant after her. Every plate carries that story. Nar Turkish Halal was the first halal platter restaurant in Madison — and it has been feeding the community with authentic, made-from-scratch Turkish food ever since.
