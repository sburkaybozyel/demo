About Us

Authentic Japanese
in the Deep South

Hachi Hachi brings genuine Japanese hibachi cooking to the heart of Magnolia. What started as a beloved food truck concept has grown into one of the most talked-about restaurants in the region — and one taste tells you exactly why.

Generous portions served fast, prices that shock you in the best way, and a Trio Plate of steak, chicken, and shrimp that customers call "the best hibachi in America." Open for dine-in, takeout, and catering.
