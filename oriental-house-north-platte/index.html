Our Story

A North Platte Institution for Over 40 Years

Liem and Ly Nguyen arrived in Nebraska in 1975 and opened their restaurant in 1983 with a simple mission: serve honest, flavorful food made with care. Decades later, that mission has never changed.

The menu spans two worlds, traditional Asian dishes like cashew chicken, lo mein, and hot and sour soup alongside American classics including charbroiled steaks, burgers, and homemade sides. Everything is made fresh, every single day.
