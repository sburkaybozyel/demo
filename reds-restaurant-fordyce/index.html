About Us

Theresa & Cliff's
Home Away from Home

Red's Restaurant is owned and run by Theresa (Red) and Cliff Harris, and it shows in every single plate. This is real, honest Southern cooking made the way it should be — fresh ingredients, generous portions, and prices that make sense.

Pies baked from scratch every morning. Chicken fried steak smothered in rich gravy. Catfish fried golden and crisp. And a cup of coffee that makes you want to stay a little longer. This is what Fordyce calls its #1 restaurant.
