About Us

Fresh Food.
Good Times. Community.

Wrecked Canoe Bar + Bistro is Clarksville's gathering place. Locally owned and community focused, it is the kind of restaurant that feels like a neighborhood — rustic barn-style setting, a covered outdoor patio, live music on weekends, and a menu built on fresh ingredients and big flavors.

From the Bacon Jam Burger to the Spicy Honey Bacon Jam Salmon, every plate reflects a commitment to quality that keeps Clarksville coming back. Breakfast through dinner, six days a week.
