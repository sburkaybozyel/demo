About Us

Warm, Fresh,
and Family-Made

Mateo's is Fordyce's go-to destination for authentic Mexican cuisine served in a warm, cozy atmosphere that feels like home. Every dish is made fresh, every guest is treated like family, and the flavors speak for themselves.

From the legendary house salsa to the chorizo chicken sizzling on its skillet, Mateo's has earned its place as one of the most beloved restaurants in the region. Dine in, drive through, or let us cater your next event.
