About Us

Afghan Flavors,
Made With Care.

The Corner Kitchen brings the rich, aromatic traditions of Afghan cuisine to Lincoln, Nebraska. Every dish is built on spices, techniques, and recipes that carry real history - the kind of food that makes people stop mid-bite and ask what is in it.

From slow-cooked chicken korma to crispy, spiced samosas and the fragrant qabili rice that regulars keep coming back for - this is food made with intention. Fresh ingredients, generous portions, and flavors Lincoln has not tasted anywhere else.
